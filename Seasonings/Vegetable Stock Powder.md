# Vegetable Stock Powder
#### Ingredients
-   2 tbsp salt
-   2 tsp dried parsley
-   1 tsp onion powder
-   1 tsp garlic powder
-   1 tsp dried thyme
-   1/2 tsp black pepper
-   1/2 tsp celery seeds
-   1 bay leaf

#### Method
-   Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly.
-   Store in an air-tight jar in a cool place out of direct sunlight.

#### To Use
-   1/2 tsp vegetable stock powder
-   1/2 tsp soy sauce
-   250mls of water.
