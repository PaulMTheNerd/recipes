# Homemade Ketchup
#### Ingredients
- 400ml passata
- 15 tsp tomato puree
- 60 ml water
- 200 ml cider vinegar
- 2 tsp onion powder
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- pinch of sea salt and black pepper

#### Method
-   Put all the ingredients in a pan and mix well.
-   Simmer on medium heat for 15 minutes.
